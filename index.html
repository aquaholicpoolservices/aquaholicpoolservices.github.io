About Aquaholic Pool & Spa Services

Aquaholic Pool & Spa Services started August 2023 as a small family business and still is to date. Servicing Durham Region, Kawartha Lakes, York Region & GTA.

Owned by Lee & Kristen Evans-Brown. With Lee having 24+ years in the industry & Kristen with education in Accounting, Small Business & Entrepreneurship, Hospitality Management. We thrive in providing the homeowner with peace of mind knowing their investments are well maintained and cared for.

What we do: Pool & spa opening/closing, weekly maintenance, water balancing, service & repairs, pool & spa covers, pool liner installations, safety cover installations & repairs, hot tub sales and more!
